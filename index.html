About

Policy. Investment.
Financial Markets.

With over a decade of experience in financial services, Pipat serves as Managing Director and Chief Economist at Kiatnakin Phatra Financial Group — one of Thailand's premier financial institutions. His work spans strategic economic insights, investment strategy, and capital market analysis that guide the organization's decision-making at the highest level.

Pipat began his career as an Economist at Thailand's Ministry of Finance, before pursuing his PhD in Economics at the University of California, Berkeley. He then joined the International Monetary Fund in Washington D.C., where he contributed to macroeconomic research and policy analysis across member nations. A 2018 Eisenhower Global Fellow, he is part of an exclusive network of exceptional leaders driving change across the world.

Before his current role, Pipat served as Chief Investment Officer of Wealth Management and sat on the Board of Directors at Kiatnakin Phatra Asset Management. He has been voted Best Thailand Economist by AsiaMoney three times — in 2020, 2022, and 2023 — and was selected as a 2018 Eisenhower Global Fellow, joining an exclusive network of outstanding leaders from around the world.
